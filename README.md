# Matéo Dib

I am a PhD Candidate at Savoie Mont Blanc University, IREGE, working in environmental and behavioral economics.
My thesis studies climate anxiety as both a psychological response to environmental degradation and a factor that can shape public attitudes, policy support, fertility intentions, and mental health.

This repository hosts my academic website.
It presents my research interests, current PhD projects, conferences and seminars, research experience, and curriculum vitae.
